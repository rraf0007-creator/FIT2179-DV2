Where do they study?

New South Wales and Victoria attract the largest numbers of international student enrolments.

What sectors do they study in?

Higher Education dominates international enrolments, followed by the VET sector.

Where do international students study in Australia?

International student enrolments are concentrated in New South Wales and Victoria, with smaller but still significant populations in Queensland, Western Australia and South Australia.

International students in the migration picture

The Department of Education data shows where international students are enrolled. ABS migration data adds another layer: how student visa arrivals and departures affect Australia's population flows.

Student visa arrivals and departures over time

Student migration dropped sharply during the border closure period, then rebounded strongly after 2021–22. The black line shows net student migration: arrivals minus departures.
