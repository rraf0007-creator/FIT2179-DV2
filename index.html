How has the sector mix changed?

Higher Education remains the largest sector, while VET has become a much larger part of international enrolments after the post-pandemic rebound.

Sector breakdown of enrolments, Jan 2026

In January 2026 YTD data, Higher Education dominates international student enrolments, followed by the VET sector.

Where do international students study in Australia?

International student enrolments are concentrated in New South Wales and Victoria, with smaller but still significant populations in Queensland, Western Australia and South Australia.

International students in the migration picture

The Department of Education data shows where international students are enrolled. ABS migration data adds another layer: how student visa arrivals and departures affect Australia's population flows.

Student visa arrivals and departures over time

Student migration dropped sharply during the border closure period, then rebounded strongly after 2021–22. The black line shows net student migration: arrivals minus departures.

Enrolments vs net migration by state

This chart combines Department enrolment data with ABS migration flow data. Larger states have the largest absolute student migration gains.

Temporary visa arrivals

In 2024–25, student visas formed the largest temporary visa migrant arrival group.

What type of student visas drive migrant arrivals?

Higher education made up the largest share of student visa migrant arrivals in 2024–25, showing that universities remain central to Australia's international student migration story.
